Adding a Description List
<dl>
<dt>List Item</dt>
<dd>More Info</dd>
<dt>List Item</dt>
<dd>More Info</dd>
</dl>
 
Makes a list where each item has a title and some extra info.

Adding a Photo
<img src="./imges/photo.png" />
 
Shows an image on your page from a file or link.

Adding a Link
<a href="http://www.fakewebsite.com">Website Name</a>
 
Creates a clickable link that takes people to another website.

Adding a Blockquote
<blockquote>
"Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua."
<cite>Author of quote</cite>
</blockquote>
 
Shows a quote from someone and can include who said it.

Adding Copyright
<footer>
<p>&copy;Copyright 2025</p>
</footer>
 
Adds a section at the bottom of your page for copyright or other info.
